Covid-19 Impact on Mental Illness

Data Analysis to see the impact of Covid-19 on mental illness amongst the different age groups in USA. Cryptocurrencies

A prominent investment bank is interested in offering a new cryptocurrency investment portfolio for its customers. The company, however, is lost in the vast universe of cryptocurrencies. Through our unsupervised machine learning analysis, we demonstrated cryptocurrencies can be clustered in groups. While there are differences between all cryptocurrencies, there are definitive categorizations by our finding. We not only have found clustering patterns, but also identified outliers. Our finding is useful to help a business venture to further explore the world of cryptocurrencies and potential investments.

Mapping Earthquakes

The analysis was done to create interactive maps using GeoJSON data maps with different layers to show the differences between the magnitudes of earthquakes all over the world for the last seven days and the tectonic plates around the globe. We used the JavaScript and the D3.js library to retrieve the coordinates and magnitudes of the earthquakes from the GeoJSON data from the USGS website. We used the Leaflet library to plot the data on a Mapbox map through an API request and create interactivity for the earthquake data.
